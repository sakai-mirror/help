Changing notification preferences

From My Workspace, you can set the email and notification preferences for your course sites, project sites, and ePortfolio sites.

Note: You cannot adjust your preferences for individual sites. For example, if you set your email notification to deliver a daily summary, you will get a daily summary message from all sites to which you belong. Or, if you choose to block low priority announcements or low priority resource notifications, these will be blocked for all your sites. You cannot block receipt of high priority notifications.

To change your email and notification preferences:

  1. On your My Workspace menubar, click Preferences, and then click Notifications.

  2. Under "Announcements", choose one of the following options:

    • Send me each notification separately
    • Send me one email per day summarizing all low priority announcements
    • Do not send me low priority announcements

  3. Under "Resources", choose one of the following options:

    • Send me each resource separately
    • Send me one email per day summarizing all low priority resource notifications
    • Do not send me low priority resource notifications

  4. Under "Syllabus", choose one of the following options:

    • Send me each notification separately
    • Send me one email per day summarizing all notifications
    • Do not send me low priority Syllabus items

  5. Under "Email Archive", choose one of the following options:

    • Send me each mail sent to site separately
    • Send me one email per day summarizing all emails
    • Do not send me emails sent to the site

  6. When you are finished, click Update Preferences to save your preferences, or click Cancel Changes to clear your changes.
